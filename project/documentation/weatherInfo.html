Weather Page

Weather page gives you an option to fetch weather information of a particular city. You can also see future weather details upto 5 days. It also gives you an option to fetch hourly weather details. Also, if you are logged in you can save your search. Saved serach will be displayed instally under bookmark tab. I reall y appreciate World Weather Online for providing the necessary API.

Details

  • This page enables you to fetch route information between two cities.
  • Provides you with google map routes.
  • You can save your search if you are logged in.
